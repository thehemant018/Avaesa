Hi, I am Mr.Arun Kapoor

I am deeply honored to have received prestigious accolades such as the

Career Started

Entering the realm of journalism in the early 1980s, I embarked on a journey fueled by curiosity, integrity, and a relentless pursuit of truth, eager to make my mark in the world of storytelling. Starting my career at Hindustan Samachar, I honed my skills and forged my path in the dynamic field of journalism.

Padma Bhushan

In 1990,I received the Padma Bhushan, one of India’s highest civilian awards, in recognition of my significant impact on Indian society and media.

Pulitzer Prize Award

In 2000,I was honored with the prestigious Pulitzer Prize Award for my outstanding contributions to journalism and public service.

Editor of Indian Express and The Times of India

My esteemed tenure as the Editor of Indian Express and The Times of India left 2005 to 2014 an indelible mark on Indian journalism, revolutionizing the industry by setting new standards for editorial excellence, journalistic integrity, and ethical reporting practices.

Minister of Communications and Information Technology (2014–2019)

I served as a Minister in the Vajpayee Ministry, overseeing crucial aspects of communication and technology during my tenure.

Retirement

After a lifetime of uncovering stories and shaping narratives, I bid farewell to my cherished career in journalism, now embracing retirement as an opportunity to impart wisdom and inspire others on their own paths toward journalistic excellence.
